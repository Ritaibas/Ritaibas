Who We Are

Design with Purpose

ARLLETICA DESIGN REPRESENTACOES E COMERCIO DE UTILIDADES DOMESTICAS LTDA is a premier commercial representative and trading house based in São Paulo. We specialize in "Utilidades Domesticas" — essential home goods that blend practical function with high-end design.

In a market often saturated with generic products, we stand as curators of quality. We connect innovative international and local manufacturers with Brazil's leading retailers and interior designers. Our mission is to elevate the everyday experience of the home through objects that are as beautiful as they are useful.

Whether acting as commercial representatives for established brands or trading exclusive collections directly, Arlletica Design ensures that every product we touch meets rigorous standards of durability, style, and market relevance.
